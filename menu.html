The menu

Yum Dog

Signature Yum Sauce, gherkins, cheese, crispy onions...if the big mac evolved into a hotdog.

The Baconator

Crispy bacon, bacon jam, bacon mayo, bacon dog and frazzles. 5 types of bacon and millions of fans. This one slaps.

The Classics

The originals. Our quality frank and bun topped with steamed mild Spanish onion, Heinz Ketchup and Frank's American Mustard. Make it more indulgent by adding cheese and bacon.

The Vegans/Veggies

Bringing the meaty goodness to the veggies and vegans. Our smoked seitan frankfurter is about as close to the real thing as you can get.

The Fries

Our high quality ziggy fries, pack in the crunch and are great for scooping up our delicious toppings. Served either with salt, blackened cajun spice, smothered in yum sauce and with bacon on top. The perfect sidekick to our super-dogs.
